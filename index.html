Welcome to
Lifelong-Ethiopia

Welcome to the Lifelong Learning Library Plc website. Our mission is to provide reading and digital literacy services to Ethiopian lifelong learners. We assist transitions from print-based reading to digital learning resources to help lifelong learners establish a solid foundation for navigating the digital world, advance the culture of reading and put their learning through the stresses of testing and applied competitive practice. Whether you are an elementary school student, a middle school student, a lifelong learner or someone seeking to expand knowledge in various subjects, this platform is designed to cater to your unique needs.

Our Values

Make Learning Fun

We believe education should be accessible to all, regardless of age, background or socio-economic status and that the highest learning achievements are best attained by fostering a love of learning and stimulating intellectual curiosity. We believe technology can be a liberating means to making education fun and accessible to all.

Value Community

We appreciate and understand the value of community and collaboration in the learning process. That is why our projects encourage user interaction and engagement on interactive platforms and live learning community events that serve community cohesion and drive emerging independent authors, researchers and educators to circulate their original works in their path to continuous self-improvement.

Build Resilience

We know the learning journey can be challenging but we are here to support your every step of the way. Our projects offer customized learning experiences. Whether you want to explore a specific subject in-depth or explore a wide-variety of disciplines; whether you wish to read, listen, watch our librarians will diligently ensure that our content is current, accurate and relevant to your information needs and inspiring to your learning aspirations.

Segenet Acadamic Support Services

Segenet

Sgenet is a permanent platform for providing learning community support services. The wide range of support services build community cohesion and provide the foundations for a culture of collaboration and continuous self-improvement among learners. Activities range from innovative anti-inactivity events promoting physical fitness for better learning and varied domain specific practice network activities in the environmental, financial literacy, aviation and engineering, geographic information systems and other emergent disciplines to addressing the simplest creature comforts like organizing safe and efficient transport services to and from learning community events.

e-Tray

is a flagship digital literacy project in our comprehensive digital literacy program. It offers elementary and middle school students and their communities opportunities for learning how to:

  • (i) Understand and practice responsible digital citizenship;
  • (ii) Develop critical thinking skills for evaluating credibility and reliability of digital learning resources, including online resources;
  • (iii) Acquire and practice effective digital communications skills and tools;
  • (iv) Acquire and practice leading strategies for online safety;
  • Develop research and information literacy skills with search engines and other digital resource.
